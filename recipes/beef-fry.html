Kerala Beef Fry (Beef Ularthiyathu)

Description

This iconic dish features tender beef chunks slow-cooked in aromatic spices and fried with coconut slices and curry leaves.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Coconut Slices
  • Shallots
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices
  • Aromatics
    •

Steps

  1. Marinate: Mix beef with ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Pressure Cook: Cook the marinated beef with 1/4 cup of water for 4 to 5 whistles until tender.
  3. Fry Coconut: Heat coconut oil in a heavy-bottomed pan and fry the coconut slices until golden brown.
  4. Sauté Aromatics: Add sliced shallots and curry leaves to the same pan, sautéing until the onions turn translucent.
  5. Combine: Pour the cooked beef along with its leftover gravy into the pan.
  6. Roast: Cook on medium-high heat until all moisture evaporates.
  7. Finish: Stir in the crushed black pepper and roast until the beef turns dark brown and dry.
